Leilani Heather is a student at Enspiral Dev Academy and a photographer based in Auckland, New Zealand. She is one half of the collaborative project Single Brown Female alongside Talia Smith.

This project explores the humour of the duo's mutual mistaken identity, and finds inspiration through shared everyday experiences with a view to subverting notions of race, identity and the gaze.
